Our Services

Specialised Pelvic Health Care

We offer a comprehensive range of pelvic floor physiotherapy services, each tailored to your individual needs.

Pelvic Floor Assessment

A thorough initial assessment including internal and external examination, muscle strength testing, and a detailed discussion of your symptoms and goals.

Comprehensive physical examination
Real-time ultrasound imaging
Personalised treatment plan
Education and self-management strategies

Postnatal Recovery

Gentle, progressive rehabilitation to help your body recover after childbirth, addressing diastasis recti, pelvic floor weakness, and pain.

Post-birth pelvic floor check
Diastasis recti assessment & rehab
Return to exercise guidance
Breastfeeding posture support

Prenatal Care

Prepare your body for birth with expert guidance on pelvic floor training, perineal massage techniques, and birthing positions.

Pelvic floor preparation
Perineal massage education
Birth positioning advice
Pregnancy-related pain management

Incontinence Treatment

Evidence-based therapy for stress, urge, and mixed incontinence. Regain control and confidence with tailored treatment programs.

Bladder retraining programs
Pelvic floor muscle training
Behavioural modification
Electrical stimulation therapy

Pelvic Pain Management

Specialised treatment for conditions such as vaginismus, vulvodynia, endometriosis-related pain, and chronic pelvic pain.

Pain neuroscience education
Manual therapy techniques
Relaxation and down-training
Graded exposure therapy

Core Rehabilitation

Rebuild your deep core system with progressive exercise programs designed to restore function and support your pelvic floor.

Deep core activation training
Progressive strengthening
Functional movement retraining
Return to sport programs
