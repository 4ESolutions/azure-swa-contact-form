About Us

Meet Dr. Emily Clarke

With over 15 years of experience in women's health physiotherapy, Dr. Emily Clarke founded Bloom Physio with a simple mission: to provide compassionate, expert pelvic floor care in a warm and welcoming environment.

After completing her Master's in Pelvic Floor Rehabilitation at the University of Melbourne, Emily has helped thousands of women regain their confidence and quality of life. She is a member of the Australian Physiotherapy Association and holds advanced certifications in women's health.

At Bloom Physio, we understand that pelvic floor issues can be deeply personal. That's why we've created a clinic that feels like a sanctuary — a place where you can feel safe, supported, and empowered on your healing journey.
